Recomendations

  • Try to maintain a single train of thought
  • Different mental tasks will produce different results for different people
  • Stare intently at a fixed point
  • Calculating math in your head (example: counting down from 100 by 7's)
  • Imagine everything you would need to pack for a long road trip or vacation
  • Silently recite the alphabet backwards
  • Translate phrases into a foreign language
  • Try not to move, blink, or talk!
