Preset sliders with example settings
  • Hover This preset is designed for an average Orbit to hover in place when fully charged. Due to variations in manufacturing, it is often desirable to fine-tune this setting using the Throttle slider. The Hover button will also reset the Throttle, Yaw, and Pitch sliders back to their defaults.
  • Forward Fly forward while hovering
  • Left Spin counter-clockwise while hovering
  • Right Spin clockwise while hovering
